FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need experience to attend classes?

Not at all! Our classes welcome all levels, from complete beginners to advanced practitioners. Our instructors provide modifications for every ability.

What should I bring to class?

Just yourself! We provide yoga mats, props, and all necessary equipment. Wear comfortable clothing you can move in.

How do I book a class?

You can book online through our scheduling system, call us directly, or drop by the studio. First-time visitors can also book a free intro session.

What is your cancellation policy?

We ask for 12 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may result in a class credit being deducted from your package.
